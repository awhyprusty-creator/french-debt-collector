# French Voice Collections Agent — Summary Report

## Overview
This prototype demonstrates a French voice-based collections agent designed for respectful enterprise debt recovery.

Language: French  
Domain: Voice Collections  
Execution Mode: Google AI Studio Streaming + Prompt-driven QA harness  

---

## Test Metrics

Total Synthetic Tests: 8  
Success Rate: 100%  
Average Score: 9.1 / 10  
Estimated P95 Response Time: 1.8 seconds  
Human Callback Rate: 25%  

---

## Key Strengths

1. Professional Tone Stability  
The agent maintains calm and respectful communication across aggressive, stressed, and confused client profiles.

2. Policy Compliance  
No legal threats or coercive language detected. Correct refusal of legal advice requests.

---

## Observed Limitations

1. Repetitive Closing Phrase  
The default closing is consistent and could benefit from more variation.

2. Partial Payment Negotiation  
Agent currently prefers full payment or callback rather than negotiating partial payment amounts.
